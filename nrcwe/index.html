Intended audience

This document is aimed at everyone working in the EU NanoSafety Cluster.

This document provides mappings for identifiers created under the name of the National Research Centre for the Working Environment (NRCWE). The source of this page can be found at https://github.com/nanocommons/specifications/nrcwe/.

Introduction

NRCWE materials have been reused in other projects and are known by other identifiers.
